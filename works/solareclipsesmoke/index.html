ECLIPSE

Interactive Smoke Sculpture

ECLIPSE Smoke Machine

"Sweet is the lore which Nature brings; Our meddling intellect, Mis-shapes the beauteous forms of things:- We murder to dissect." - The Tables Turned (William Wordsworth)

ECLIPSE is a new concept that turns the idea of ‘interactive’ art on its head. Simply - the closer a viewer gets to the sculpture, the more it begins to shut down.

ECLIPSE takes advantage of our obsession with technology. However, unlike modern technology, ECLIPSE does NOT allow us to immerse ourselves within it. We are instead encouraged to stand back, to observe, and take a moment in our own reality… Something that has become a rare moment in the modern world.

It will take the viewer a moment to realise that it is their own “meddling” which is destroying the piece in front of them. Only when they stand back will they gain a better perspective.

ECLIPSE shutdown

ECLIPSE is a glowing ‘smoking’ ring of light that has a calming ethereal feel, much like the solar eclipse. Its comprised of LED lights and custom-built smoke machines that emanate a ‘mist’ from perforated black tubing. (The smoke is made from atomising a glycerin : distilled water solution. This is a non-toxic, safe and widely used smoke solution.)

Motion sensors, electronic relays and a microcontroller are used to control and monitor the state of the sculpture. It’s programmed to “switch off” its LED lights and smoke machine pumps when viewers come within a certain range.
